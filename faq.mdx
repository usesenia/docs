These are the questions clinics most commonly ask before and after getting started with Senia.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Will my receptionist still be needed?">
    Yes — and they'll work better than ever. Senia takes on the repetitive, high-volume work: confirming appointments, answering frequently asked questions, and sending return reminders. Your receptionist stays free for everything that requires judgment, empathy, and the institutional knowledge that only comes from knowing your clinic firsthand.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Senia work with my clinic management system?">
    Senia integrates with the leading clinic management systems in Brazil, including Feegow, Clinicorp, Gestão DS, Visual Asa, App Health, and Controle Odonto. If your system has an open API, Senia can connect to it. Clinics that don't use an integrated management system can use Senia's own built-in digital agenda at no extra cost.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will patients know they're talking to an AI?">
    Senia is trained to communicate like a professional receptionist, using the name, tone, and protocols you define. When a conversation requires a human touch, Senia transfers it to your team along with the full conversation context — so your staff can pick up right where Senia left off, without asking the patient to repeat themselves.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does onboarding work?">
    Senia's team guides you through the entire process. On Day 1, you walk the team through how your clinic operates — your specialty, schedules, health plans, and service protocols — and Senia is trained to respond exactly the way you would. During the first week, hundreds of automated test scenarios are run and you review the results before anything goes live. By Week 2, Senia is handling patient conversations end to end. Onboarding is fully assisted; you're never on your own.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription?">
    Yes, at any time and without penalty. There are no cancellation fees, no lock-in periods, and no paperwork. You can cancel directly from the admin panel and your access remains active until the end of the billing period you've already paid for. After cancellation, you have 30 days to export your data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to patient data?">
    Patient data is stored securely and used exclusively to operate the service for your clinic. Senia never sells or shares patient data with third parties for commercial purposes. All data is protected with TLS encryption in transit and row-level security in the database, meaning each clinic's data is completely isolated. For a full breakdown of what is stored and for how long, see the [Privacy & Data](/privacy-and-data) page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if Senia gives a wrong answer?">
    Senia uses a knowledge base built from the information you provide during onboarding, and its responses can be updated at any time. If a patient receives an incorrect answer, you can find the conversation in the Conversations tab, identify the problematic response, and correct the knowledge base in Settings to prevent the same error from recurring. Because Senia is an AI, the Terms of Service note that you remain responsible for reviewing and validating critical interactions with patients.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum contract period?">
    No. All Senia plans are available without a loyalty contract or minimum commitment. You can subscribe month to month and cancel whenever you choose. Annual billing is available and may offer different pricing, but it is never a requirement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What specialties does Senia support?">
    Senia is designed to work across a wide range of medical and health specialties. Clinics currently using Senia include those focused on dentistry, aesthetics, orthopedics, ophthalmology, psychology, physiotherapy, and integrative medicine, among others. Because Senia is trained on your clinic's specific protocols, it adapts to the workflows and terminology of whatever specialty you practice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages does Senia communicate in?">
    Senia is designed for Brazilian clinics and communicates with patients in Portuguese. The tone, vocabulary, and communication style are all configured during onboarding to match your clinic's standards and the expectations of your patient base.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What do I need to get started?">
    To get started, you need a legally registered clinic or health establishment, a WhatsApp Business number for patient communication, and access to your clinic management system's API credentials if you want the full scheduling integration. Senia's team handles the technical setup — you provide the information about how your clinic works, and they do the rest. No technical background is required on your end.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is pricing determined?">
    Senia's pricing is proportional to the size of your clinic, calculated based on the number of patients you see per month. All plans include assisted onboarding at no extra charge. Senia's team will recommend the right plan for your specific situation — reach out via WhatsApp at +55 21 98474-1310 to get a recommendation.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
