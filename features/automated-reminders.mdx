One of the most impactful things Senia does is send timely reminders — reducing no-shows and ensuring patients return for follow-up treatments without your team needing to remember. Once configured, reminders go out automatically for every appointment and every return interval you define, at any hour, without any manual effort.

## Appointment reminders

Senia sends appointment reminders automatically in the lead-up to each scheduled visit. By default, reminders go out the day before the appointment and again on the morning of the appointment — though you can adjust these timings to match your clinic's workflow.

Each reminder message includes the appointment date, time, professional's name, and any preparation instructions relevant to the visit. The patient can respond directly in the chat to confirm they are coming, ask to reschedule, or cancel — and Senia handles all three responses automatically, updating the calendar without any intervention from your team.

## Return reminders

For clinics where multi-session treatments are common — physiotherapy, aesthetics, orthodontics, integrative medicine, and others — patient return rates directly affect both treatment outcomes and revenue. Senia sends follow-up messages at intervals you define, prompting patients to book their next session before the gap becomes too long.

One clinic using Senia for physiotherapy and integrative treatments put it plainly: *"Our treatments require patient returns to work. Before, that depended on our receptionist remembering — and sometimes it didn't happen. With Senia, reminders go out automatically and return visits have become routine."*

You can configure different return intervals for different services — for example, a one-week reminder for physiotherapy patients and a four-week reminder after a cosmetic procedure. Each reminder can carry a personalised message appropriate for that treatment type.

## Configuring reminders

All reminder settings are managed in **Platform → Settings**. From there you can:

- Set the timing of appointment reminders (e.g. 24 hours before, 2 hours before)
- Define return reminder intervals per service or specialty
- Edit the message templates used for each reminder type
- Enable or disable individual reminder flows

Changes take effect immediately for all future reminders.

<Tip>
  Personalise your reminder message templates in Settings to match your clinic's tone. A warm, on-brand reminder message feels like a personal touch from your team — not a generic notification.
</Tip>

## Why reminders matter

The business case for automated reminders is straightforward:

- **Fewer no-shows** mean fewer empty slots and more predictable daily revenue
- **Higher return rates** keep patients on multi-session treatment plans that produce real clinical outcomes
- **Full appointment slots** mean your professionals' time is used at its highest value
- **Automated follow-ups** remove a repetitive manual task from your receptionist's workload entirely

Clinics in specialties with frequent return visits — physiotherapy, aesthetics, orthodontics — often see the biggest impact within the first month of using Senia.

<Note>
  Patients can reply to any reminder to confirm, reschedule, or cancel. Senia handles all three flows automatically: confirmations are logged, reschedules are processed in your calendar, and cancellations free up the slot — all without your team lifting a finger.
</Note>
