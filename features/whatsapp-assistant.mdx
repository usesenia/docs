Senia connects directly to your WhatsApp Business number and responds to patient messages 24/7 using the protocols and communication style you define during onboarding. From the moment a patient sends their first message, Senia is there — answering questions, booking appointments, and representing your clinic with the same professionalism your team brings every day.

## What Senia handles on WhatsApp

Senia is trained on your clinic's specific information and can manage a wide range of patient interactions without any intervention from your team:

- **Frequently asked questions** — clinic hours, location, services offered, and insurance plans accepted
- **Appointment booking** — checking availability and confirming new appointments in real time
- **Rescheduling** — handling patient requests to move an existing appointment to a new slot
- **Cancellations** — processing cancellation requests and freeing up the slot in your calendar
- **Insurance inquiries** — clarifying which health plans and insurers your clinic works with
- **Post-appointment instructions** — sharing follow-up care guidance after a visit
- **Return reminders** — prompting patients who need follow-up sessions to book their next appointment

## Human handoff

Not every conversation can or should be handled by an AI. When Senia detects that a situation requires human judgment — a sensitive complaint, a complex clinical question, or a patient who specifically asks to speak with a person — she transfers the conversation to your team immediately.

Your receptionist picks up the handoff seamlessly: the full conversation history is already visible, so there is no need to ask the patient to repeat themselves. Your team arrives in the chat with complete context and can continue the conversation naturally, exactly as if they had been there from the start.

## Senia's tone and persona

During onboarding, you choose the name and communication style Senia uses with your patients. She can sign messages as "Senia", or as any name that fits your clinic's brand — the same name your human receptionists use when messaging patients.

Senia is trained to sound like a professional, warm, and attentive receptionist. Many patients interact with her without ever realising they are speaking with an AI, and that is intentional: it ensures every patient receives a consistent, polished experience regardless of when they reach out or how busy your team is.

<Note>
  Senia does **not** give medical advice or make any clinical decisions. She handles administrative and scheduling tasks only. Any question that requires clinical judgement is escalated to your team through the human handoff flow.
</Note>

## Outside business hours

When your office is closed, Senia keeps working. Patients who message at night, on weekends, or during public holidays receive immediate, accurate responses — not an automated "we're closed" message, but a real conversation that can book their appointment, answer their question, or route them to your team when they are back online.

No message goes unanswered. No appointment opportunity is lost.

<Tip>
  Keep Senia's persona name consistent with how your human receptionists sign their WhatsApp messages. A seamless transition between Senia and your team feels natural to patients and reinforces trust in your clinic's communication.
</Tip>
