## How Senia works

Senia connects to your WhatsApp Business number and your existing clinic management system. From the moment she goes live, she handles the routine volume — so your receptionist can focus on patients who are physically in the clinic.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tell Senia about your clinic">
    Share your specialty, schedule, insurance plans, and service protocols. Senia learns your clinic's voice and rules during the onboarding session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We run automated tests">
    Before going live, our team runs hundreds of simulated patient scenarios to make sure Senia responds correctly in every situation. You review and approve.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Senia starts handling patient messages on WhatsApp. Appointments are confirmed, reminders go out automatically, and your team gets a notification whenever a conversation needs human attention.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor from the dashboard">
    Track appointments, active conversations, and key metrics from the Senia platform — all in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>
