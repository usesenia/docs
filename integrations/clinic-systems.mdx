Senia integrates with the most popular clinic management systems in Brazil so that appointments booked through WhatsApp appear instantly in your existing system — and Senia always works from up-to-date availability. Your staff never has to copy bookings between tools, and patients always see accurate scheduling options.

## Supported systems

| System | Notes |
|---|---|
| Feegow | Full two-way sync |
| Clinicorp | Full two-way sync |
| Gestão DS | Full two-way sync |
| App Health | Full two-way sync |
| Controle Odonto | Full two-way sync |
| Visual Asa (Tecnoarte) | Full two-way sync |
| Any system with open API | Custom integration |

## How the sync works

When a patient books an appointment through WhatsApp, Senia writes that appointment directly to your clinic management system — including the patient's name, the service, the professional, and the time. Your staff sees the booking in the system they already use, exactly as if the receptionist had entered it manually.

The sync works in both directions. Cancellations and reschedules initiated through WhatsApp are propagated to your clinic system automatically. If your staff modifies or cancels an appointment from within the clinic system, Senia picks up the change and reflects the updated availability to patients.

## Open API integration {#open-api}

For systems not in the list above, Senia can integrate with any clinic management platform that provides an open REST API. The process is handled by the Senia technical team — you don't need to write any code or manage the connection yourself.

To start a custom integration, contact the Senia team and share your system's API documentation. The team will review the available endpoints, confirm what data can be exchanged, and set up the connection during your onboarding period.

## Setting up the connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share your API credentials">
    During onboarding, provide your clinic management system's API credentials to the Senia team. The team will tell you exactly what information is needed for your specific system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The team configures the sync">
    The Senia team sets up the integration on the backend, maps the relevant data fields, and runs a series of tests to confirm that appointments, cancellations, and reschedules are flowing correctly in both directions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You confirm everything looks right">
    Review a set of test appointments in both your clinic system and the Senia platform. Once you confirm the data matches, the integration goes live for your patients.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You are responsible for ensuring you have the necessary authorization to connect your clinic management system to third-party services, in accordance with your software agreement with that provider.
</Note>
