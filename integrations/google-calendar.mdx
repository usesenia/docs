Senia's Google Calendar integration lets your professionals see their upcoming appointments in the calendar they already use, without logging into the Senia platform. Whether they're checking their phone between consultations or planning the week ahead on a desktop, their Senia schedule is always right there in Google Calendar.

## What the integration does

Appointments confirmed through Senia appear automatically in the linked Google Calendar — no manual entry required. If a patient cancels or reschedules through WhatsApp, the calendar event is updated in real time. Your professionals always have an accurate view of their day without needing to cross-reference multiple systems.

## Setting up Google Calendar sync

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integrations settings">
    Go to **Settings > Integrations** in the Senia platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your Google account">
    Click **Connect Google Calendar** and sign in with the Google account associated with the calendar you want to sync.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant calendar permissions">
    Approve the permissions prompt so Senia can create and manage calendar events on your behalf. Senia only writes appointment events — it does not read or modify any other calendar content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose which professionals to sync">
    Select the professionals whose schedules should be linked to Google Calendar. You can connect one calendar per professional.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the test appointment">
    Book a test appointment in Senia and verify that it appears correctly in Google Calendar with the right time, duration, and details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Per-professional calendars

Each professional at your clinic can have their own Google Calendar linked to their Senia schedule. When an appointment is booked, Senia creates the event in the calendar belonging to the assigned professional — so every team member sees only their own bookings, not the entire clinic's schedule.

Professionals can connect their calendars themselves by following the setup steps above from their own Senia login, or a clinic administrator can set up the connections on their behalf from the Integrations settings.

## What shows up in Google Calendar

Each appointment event created by Senia includes:

- **Title:** Patient name and service type (for example, *Maria Silva — Initial Consultation*)
- **Time and duration:** Exact start and end time as booked in Senia
- **Link:** A direct link back to the full patient conversation in the Senia platform, so professionals can review the context before the appointment

<Note>
  Senia reads your Google Calendar to check availability and writes to it for confirmed appointments. Senia does not read, modify, or delete any calendar events that were not created by Senia itself — your existing personal or professional events are untouched.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Encourage each professional to connect their personal Google Calendar so their Senia appointments are always visible on their phone. It takes less than two minutes and means they never need to open Senia just to check what's next.
</Tip>
