## Clinics without a management system

Not every clinic runs on a dedicated management platform — and that's fine. Senia includes a built-in digital agenda at no extra cost. You can start scheduling appointments, managing availability, and automating patient communication from day one, without integrating any third-party system. If you add a clinic management system later, the Senia team can connect it at any time.

## How integrations are set up

The Senia team handles the technical side of every integration during your onboarding. You provide your API credentials for the relevant system, and the team configures the connection, tests the data flow, and confirms that appointments are syncing correctly in both directions. You don't need technical knowledge to get a Senia integration running.

Your credentials are stored securely using industry-standard practices and are never shared with third parties outside the scope of the integration itself.

<Note>
  New integrations are added regularly based on customer demand. If your clinic management system isn't listed, contact the Senia team — if it has an open API, a connection is very likely possible.
</Note>
