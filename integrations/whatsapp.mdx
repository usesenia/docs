Senia uses the WhatsApp Business API — provided by Meta — to connect to your clinic's WhatsApp number. Once connected, Senia handles incoming patient messages automatically, scheduling appointments, answering common questions, and sending reminders around the clock. Your team keeps full visibility over every conversation from the Conversations section of the Senia platform.

## What you need

Before the Senia team can connect your WhatsApp number, make sure you have the following in place:

- An active WhatsApp Business account registered to your clinic
- Your clinic's WhatsApp number — this can be a dedicated line or your existing business number
- Permission to connect that number to the WhatsApp Business API (Meta requires a brief verification process for new API connections)

## How the connection works

The Senia team guides you through the WhatsApp Business API verification process as part of your onboarding. You don't need to manage the technical setup yourself — the team handles the configuration and liaises with Meta on your behalf.

Once Meta approves the connection, Senia is linked to your number and ready to receive messages. The verification and approval process typically takes **1–3 business days** from the time you submit your information.

## What changes after connecting

After Senia is connected, all incoming messages to that WhatsApp number are handled by Senia first. The AI reads each message, determines the intent, and responds — booking appointments, confirming details, or answering questions — without any action required from your team.

When a conversation needs human attention (for example, a complex clinical question or a situation outside Senia's scope), your team receives a notification and can step in directly from the Senia Conversations panel. The patient stays in the same WhatsApp thread and sees a seamless handoff.

## Using a new vs. existing number

You have two options for which number to use with Senia:

**Your existing business WhatsApp number** — patients who already have your number saved can message Senia without any change on their end. This is the lowest-friction option for established clinics.

**A new, dedicated number** — setting up a separate number for Senia keeps AI-handled traffic distinct from any other communication channels your team uses. This can simplify reporting and make it easier to track Senia's activity independently.

Either approach works well. The Senia team can advise on which option suits your clinic's setup during onboarding.

<Warning>
  Once Senia is connected to a WhatsApp Business number, that number cannot also be used in the standard WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app simultaneously. The WhatsApp Business API takes exclusive control of the number. Plan your number assignment before starting the connection process.
</Warning>

<Note>
  WhatsApp Business API usage is included in your Senia subscription. You do not need a separate billing relationship with Meta to use the API through Senia.
</Note>
