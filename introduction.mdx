Senia is an AI-powered virtual receptionist built specifically for medical clinics in Brazil. It works alongside your human receptionist on WhatsApp — answering patient messages, confirming appointments, sending reminders, and following your clinic's protocols — around the clock, every day of the year. While your team focuses on in-person care and the conversations that require real human judgment, Senia handles the volume that would otherwise pile up in a chat queue.

## How Senia helps your clinic

Every clinic loses revenue and staff energy to the same four problems. Senia addresses each one from day one.

**24/7 patient coverage** — Patients don't stop needing answers when your doors close. Senia responds to WhatsApp messages during evenings, weekends, and holidays so no message goes unanswered and no appointment opportunity is missed.

**A freer, more focused receptionist** — When Senia absorbs repetitive, high-volume tasks — confirming schedules, answering common questions, collecting deposits — your receptionist reclaims time for work that genuinely requires a human: greeting patients, handling sensitive situations, and making judgment calls.

**Protocols followed every single time** — Senia is trained on your clinic's specific rules. Whether that means collecting a booking deposit via Pix before confirming a slot, or routing certain specialties through a pre-screening flow, the protocol runs correctly on every conversation — not just when your team remembers.

**Fewer no-shows, more revenue** — Automated appointment confirmations and return-visit reminders mean patients are less likely to forget or skip. Clinics using Senia report that follow-up care becomes routine rather than something that depends on a staff member remembering to send a message.

## What Senia handles

Senia manages the full patient messaging lifecycle on WhatsApp:

- Answering frequently asked questions (hours, location, accepted insurance, service descriptions) using a customizable knowledge base you control
- Scheduling, rescheduling, and cancelling appointments according to your availability rules
- Sending appointment confirmation messages immediately after booking
- Collecting booking deposits or pre-registration information when your protocol requires it
- Sending automatic reminders before appointments and follow-up messages for return visits
- Handing off to a human team member — with full conversation context — when the situation calls for it

<Note>
  Senia operates under the name and tone of voice you define during onboarding. Patients experience a professional receptionist; you decide whether that persona is named "Senia" or something that matches your clinic's brand.
</Note>

## Integrations with your existing systems

Senia connects directly to the clinic management systems you already use — including Feegow, Clinicorp, Gestão DS, App Health, Controle Odonto, and Visual Asa. If your system exposes an open API, Senia can integrate with it. Clinics that don't use any management platform get access to Senia's built-in digital agenda at no extra cost.

## Getting started

Setting up Senia takes roughly one week. A dedicated Senia specialist guides you through the entire process — from training the AI on your clinic's specific information to running a full automated test battery before a single real patient ever sends a message. Assisted onboarding is included in all plans.

## What Senia does NOT replace

Senia is designed to complement your team, not eliminate it.

- **Clinical decisions** — Senia never gives medical advice, interprets symptoms, or recommends treatments. Any conversation that touches clinical territory is immediately escalated to your team.
- **Human judgment in complex situations** — Distressed patients, billing disputes, complaints, and sensitive personal situations are handed off to a human with the full conversation transcript in hand.
- **In-person reception** — Senia works exclusively over WhatsApp. Everything that happens at your front desk — checking in patients, handling walk-ins, managing the waiting room — remains with your staff.

<Tip>
  The clinics that get the most out of Senia are the ones that let it own the repetitive volume completely, so their receptionists can do the high-judgment work that only a person can do well.
</Tip>
