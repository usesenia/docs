The Senia onboarding process spans roughly one week and is divided into three phases: training the AI on your clinic's specific information, running a full automated test battery, and going live on your WhatsApp Business number. The Senia team guides you through every phase — you will never be handed a manual and left to configure things on your own.

<Note>
  Assisted onboarding is included in all Senia plans at no extra cost. You will have a dedicated specialist from the first setup session through your go-live date.
</Note>

## Day 1 — AI training

On your first day, you sit down (virtually or in person) with a Senia specialist for a structured training session. The goal is to transfer your clinic's knowledge — every rule, preference, and workflow — into Senia's configuration.

Come prepared to share the following:

- **Specialty and services** — the treatments and procedures you offer, any intake requirements, and how patients should inquire about each one
- **Schedule and availability** — working hours for each professional, appointment durations, and any scheduling rules (e.g., new patients vs. returning patients, minimum advance booking time)
- **Insurance and payment rules** — which health plans you accept, self-pay pricing, deposit amounts, Pix keys or payment links, and what to do when a patient's plan is not accepted
- **Staff names and roles** — the name and specialty of each professional so Senia can direct patients accurately and hand off conversations to the right person
- **Service protocols and business rules** — any step-by-step workflows Senia must follow, such as requesting a deposit before confirming an appointment slot, sending a pre-consultation form, or applying different booking flows for specific procedures
- **Knowledge base content** — answers to your most common patient questions (hours, location, accepted plans, pricing, service descriptions) so Senia responds exactly as you would from day one
- **Tone, voice, and persona** — whether your clinic communicates formally or informally, the name the virtual receptionist will use, and any brand language to include or avoid

<Tip>
  Prepare a list of your 20 most common patient questions before the training session. Covering real questions from your own patients — not hypothetical ones — is the fastest way to ensure Senia sounds exactly like your clinic from day one.
</Tip>

By the end of Day 1, Senia's configuration reflects your clinic's complete operational profile. The Senia team reviews it internally before moving to the testing phase.

## Week 1 — Automated test battery

Before Senia speaks to a single real patient, the Senia team runs hundreds of simulated patient scenarios against your configuration. This is not a manual spot-check — it is a comprehensive, automated battery designed to surface any gap between how Senia was configured and how it should actually behave.

The test battery covers:

- **Appointment booking flows** — patients requesting new appointments, preferred times, specific professionals, and specialty-based routing
- **FAQ responses** — questions about hours, location, accepted insurance plans, pricing, and service descriptions
- **Protocol edge cases** — scenarios where the booking rules are triggered: deposit requests, unavailable slots, professionals on leave, after-hours inquiries that require a callback
- **After-hours messages** — what Senia says when patients write outside of business hours and how it manages their expectations
- **Human handoff triggers** — conversations that escalate to a team member, and whether Senia passes along the correct context

You receive the full transcript of the test battery to review at your own pace. If any response is off — wrong tone, incorrect protocol, missing information — the Senia team adjusts the configuration and reruns the affected scenarios. You approve the final results before anything goes live.

## Week 2 — Going live

Once you approve the test battery, Senia connects to your WhatsApp Business number. From that moment, every inbound patient message is handled automatically.

Here is what the patient experience looks like end to end:

1. **Patient sends a message** — the patient writes to your clinic's WhatsApp number at any time of day
2. **Senia responds immediately** — the patient receives a reply within seconds, in the tone and language of your clinic
3. **Appointment confirmed** — if the patient wants to book, Senia checks availability, applies your scheduling rules, and confirms the slot (collecting a deposit if your protocol requires it)
4. **Reminder sent automatically** — before the appointment date, Senia sends a confirmation reminder without any action required from your team
5. **Return visit reminder** — after the appointment, Senia sends follow-up messages to bring patients back for their next scheduled visit, making return care routine rather than dependent on staff memory
6. **Human handoff when needed** — if the conversation requires a real person (a clinical question, a complaint, a situation Senia is not configured to handle), Senia transfers the chat to your team with the full conversation history attached

Your receptionist arrives each morning with the agenda already organized and the overnight messages already answered.

## After going live

Going live is not the end of the process — it is the beginning of an improving system.

**Monitor via the dashboard** — your Senia dashboard gives you a real-time view of active conversations, scheduled appointments, and key performance indicators. You can review any conversation at any time.

**Retrain Senia as your clinic evolves** — when you add a new service, change your pricing, update your insurance list, or hire a new professional, share the update with the Senia team and the configuration is updated. Senia learns continuously.

**Add or remove professionals and services** — your Senia configuration is not static. As your team changes, your scheduling rules and availability can be updated quickly through the Senia team without downtime.

**Review and refine responses** — if a real patient conversation reveals a gap (an unusual question Senia answered imperfectly, a new protocol edge case), flag it in the dashboard and the Senia team will retrain the model on that scenario.
