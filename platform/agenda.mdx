The Agenda section is where you manage your clinic's schedule. Senia reads availability from the Agenda in real time, so the appointments patients book through WhatsApp are always accurate — no double-bookings, no slots offered for professionals who are on leave, and no appointments scheduled outside your configured working hours.

## Adding professionals

Each professional at your clinic needs a profile in the Agenda before Senia can book appointments on their behalf.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Professionals panel">
    In the Agenda section, select **Professionals** from the left menu, then click **Add professional**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the professional's details">
    Fill in the following fields:

    - **Full name** — as you want it to appear to patients during booking.
    - **Specialty** — for example, Physiotherapy, Dentistry, or Psychology.
    - **CRM / professional registration number** — required for medical professionals in Brazil.
    - **Profile photo** (optional) — shown in the booking flow so patients recognise who they are booking with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set working hours">
    Define the professional's availability for each day of the week. For each active day, set a start time and an end time. You can configure a lunch break or mid-day gap by splitting the day into two time blocks — for example, 08:00–12:00 and 14:00–18:00.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the profile">
    Click **Save**. The professional is immediately available for scheduling. Senia will start offering their slots to patients as soon as you link them to at least one service.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuring services

Services define what patients can book. Each service is linked to one or more professionals so Senia knows who to schedule when a patient requests it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Services panel">
    In the Agenda section, select **Services**, then click **Add service**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the service details">
    Complete the following fields:

    - **Service name** — for example, Initial Consultation, Return Visit, or Acupuncture Session.
    - **Duration** — how long the appointment lasts, in minutes. Senia uses this to block the correct amount of time in the professional's diary.
    - **Associated professionals** — select every professional who performs this service. Senia will offer available slots across all of them.
    - **Price** — the patient-facing price for this service.
    - **Deposit required** — toggle on if you require a deposit to confirm the booking. Enter the deposit amount and the PIX key or payment instructions that Senia will send automatically after the patient chooses a slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the service">
    Click **Save**. The service is immediately available in the booking flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you require a deposit, Senia sends the payment instructions automatically at the end of the booking conversation — no manual follow-up needed. The appointment is held as **pending confirmation** until the deposit is received or you confirm it manually.
</Info>

## Setting availability

Beyond daily working hours, you can fine-tune each professional's schedule to handle real-world complexity.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add exceptions">
    Use the **Exceptions** calendar to block specific dates — public holidays, vacation periods, or one-off absences. Senia will not offer any slots on blocked dates, even if they fall within the professional's regular working hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure buffer time">
    Set a buffer period between appointments to give professionals time to prepare, clean the room, or update patient notes. For example, a 10-minute buffer after a 50-minute session means the next slot opens at the 60-minute mark, not the 50-minute mark.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a booking window">
    Define how far in advance patients can book. For example, you can allow bookings up to 60 days ahead and no less than 2 hours before the appointment time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Changes to availability take effect immediately — Senia will only offer patients slots that match the updated schedule. If you need to block a date urgently, add the exception and it will apply to all new conversations at once.
</Note>

## Built-in agenda vs. your own system

Clinics without a third-party management system can use Senia's built-in digital agenda at no extra cost. It supports multiple professionals, multiple services, exception dates, buffer times, and deposit workflows — everything covered in this guide. You do not need an external system to use any of Senia's scheduling features.

If you later adopt a third-party system, migrating from Senia's built-in agenda to a connected system is straightforward. Contact the Senia support team and they will guide you through the transition without loss of historical data.
