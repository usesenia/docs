Senia goes live in roughly one week, and the Senia team is with you at every step. There is no software to install, no engineering team required, and no long-term contract to sign before you start. This page walks you through the three phases — from your first demo call to the moment your patients start booking appointments automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book a demo">
    Reach out to the Senia team directly on WhatsApp to schedule a free demonstration. During this call, a Senia specialist walks you through the platform, answers your questions, and helps you understand what the setup process looks like for your specific clinic type and size.

    You are not committing to anything by booking a demo. There is no contract required to get started, and you can cancel at any time after you go live — no penalty, no lock-in period.

    After the demo, if you decide to move forward, the Senia team schedules your onboarding session and you move to Step 2 immediately.

    <Info>
      Pricing is calculated based on the number of patients your clinic serves per month, so the Senia team will recommend the right plan during the demo call.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Train Senia on your clinic">
    This is where Senia becomes *your* receptionist. In a structured onboarding session, you share everything Senia needs to represent your clinic correctly:

    - **Specialty and services** — the treatments and procedures you offer, including any pre-screening or eligibility requirements
    - **Schedule and availability** — which professionals work which days, session lengths, and any scheduling rules (e.g., first appointments vs. follow-ups)
    - **Insurance and payment** — which health plans you accept, self-pay pricing, deposit requirements, and Pix keys
    - **Staff names and roles** — so Senia can direct patients to the right professional and hand off conversations accurately
    - **Protocols and business rules** — your specific workflows, such as requiring a deposit before confirming a slot or routing certain specialties through a pre-screening step
    - **Knowledge base** — the answers to your most common patient questions, so Senia responds about hours, location, accepted plans, and services exactly as you would
    - **Tone and voice** — whether your clinic communicates formally or warmly, and what name the virtual receptionist should use

    Senia learns your clinic's rules and applies them consistently to every patient conversation — no exceptions, no forgotten steps.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the test battery and go live">
    Before any real patient ever sends a message, the Senia team runs hundreds of automated test scenarios against your configuration. These simulations cover appointment booking, FAQ responses, protocol edge cases, after-hours messages, and human handoff triggers.

    You receive the full transcript of the test battery to review. Once you are satisfied that Senia is responding correctly in every scenario, you approve the go-live. Senia then connects to your WhatsApp Business number and starts handling patient conversations automatically — including scheduling confirmations, reminders, return-visit follow-ups, and seamless handoffs to your team when a situation requires a real person.

    From that point forward, your clinic's WhatsApp works around the clock — including evenings, weekends, and public holidays.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you need to get started

You do not need a technical background or a dedicated IT resource. Before your onboarding session, gather the following:

- **A WhatsApp Business number** — this is the number Senia will operate on. If you do not have one yet, the Senia team will guide you through creating it.
- **Your clinic management system** — if you use a platform like Feegow, Clinicorp, Gestão DS, App Health, Controle Odonto, or Visual Asa, Senia can connect directly to it. If you do not use any system, Senia includes a built-in digital agenda at no extra cost.
- **A list of your services and professionals** — names, specialties, schedule, and any rules that govern how patients book with each one.

<Note>
  No technical integration is required on day one. The Senia team handles all configuration and connection to your existing systems. You provide the information; they handle the setup.
</Note>
